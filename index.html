Our Founding Story

Beej Network is an angel investing network that provided financial aid to qualified startups through integrated resources. This in turn fills the gap in the financial early-stage funding ecosystem. We aim to help startups raise funds and grow their operations while increasing profits for all stakeholders involved.

The co-founders Nikhil Jain, Nikhil Sethia, K.V.T. Ramesh, and P.S. Suman decided to establish Beej Network with one goal, to bring leverage to revolutionary startups. The focus is on building a high-value generating ecosystem. Beej’s Angel Network is building a bridge that facilitates big-ticket investments for high-growth startups. At the same time, they also encourage first-gen investors new to the idea of startup investing.
